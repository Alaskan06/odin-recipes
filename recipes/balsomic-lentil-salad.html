Balsamic Lentil Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked lentils, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the lentil mixture and toss gently to combine.
  4. Let the salad sit for at least 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired before serving.
