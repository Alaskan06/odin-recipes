Fennel Caper Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 fennel bulb, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons capers, rinsed and drained
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced fennel and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Push the fennel to the side of the skillet and add the salmon fillets, skin-side down.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then sprinkle capers over the top.
  6. Cook for about 6-8 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through.
  7. Drizzle with lemon juice before serving.
