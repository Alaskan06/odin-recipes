Lemon Rosted Asparagus

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the asparagus on a baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then sprinkle with lemon zest, salt, pepper, and garlic powder if using.
  4. Toss to coat the asparagus evenly.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until tender and slightly crispy.
  6. Serve warm as a side dish.
